
## Dependencies

Writing last versions of dependencies with which it was possible to make it happen:

- `Kubuntu 22.04.3 LTS`
    - CPU: Intel i5-10400 (12) @ 4.300GHz
    - GPU: Intel CometLake-S GT2 [UHD Graphics 630]
- `lutris/now 0.5.16 all [installed,upgradable to: 0.5.17]` ( output of `$ apt list --installed | grep "lutris"`)
- [Proton Wine 10.0.4](<https://github.com/Kron4ek/Wine-Builds/releases/download/proton-10.0-4/wine-proton-10.0-4-amd64.tar.xz>)
- wine gecko (32) 2.47.4 was installed by default! if u don't have one, Disco launcher at least may not work. You may need to do it with commands like (pacman -S wine-gecko) if u are on arch for example
